The Green Edge

4.5
100% Vegan Bistro Café Deli Ice Cream Groceries

The Green Edge is a unique all-in-one vegan destination in Brisbane, combining a bistrô, café, deli, juice bar, ice cream shop, and grocery store. It offers a fully plant-based experience—from casual dining and specialty coffee to fresh juices, desserts, and a wide range of vegan products—making it a go-to spot for both eating and shopping in one place.
