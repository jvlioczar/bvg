About the Project

For the animals, always

The Project

First and foremost, this site is dedicated to all animals — those exploited by humans and those suffering across the planet. This project exists, above all, to help them.

Secondly, this guide was created to help vegans, those looking to reduce their negative impact on animal lives, and anyone interested in plant-based eating from around the world to find the best places in Brisbane for a great meal.

The guide provides an independent, quick, and objective assessment — based on a wide range of reviews — of fully vegan restaurants and cafés, followed by a list of vegan-friendly venues.

Food is the largest driver of animal exploitation. It is essential that vegan businesses are promoted and that they continually improve, so that more people can enjoy the comfort of eating and gathering without harming animals of other species.

The project also showcases groups and key figures in the animal movement who contribute or have contributed to the cause in Brisbane and surrounds.
