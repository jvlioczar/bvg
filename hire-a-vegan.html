Hire a Vegan

Solidarity economy, cruelty-free

A directory of vegan professionals in Brisbane — Meanjin. Support the community, hire with purpose.

What is this?

Hire a Vegan is a simple idea with a powerful impact: a directory of vegan professionals in Brisbane offering their skills and services to the community.

When you hire a vegan, you're doing more than getting a job done — you're strengthening a network of people who share a commitment to reducing animal suffering. You're keeping money within a community that actively works towards a kinder world.

From graphic designers to personal trainers, photographers to electricians, tutors to accountants — whatever you need, check here first. And if you can't find what you're looking for, let us know. We're always growing.

Every time you choose a vegan professional, you're voting for a more compassionate economy.
Want to be listed?
How to add your name
  1. You must be vegan and based in Brisbane or surrounds.
  2. Send us a DM on Instagram with: your full name, your profession or service, your social media link (Instagram, website, LinkedIn, etc.), and your email.
  3. We'll review and add you to the directory. It's free and always will be.
